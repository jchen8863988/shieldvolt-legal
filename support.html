ShieldVolt is built and maintained by an individual developer. Every support request is read and answered personally — we ask for up to two weeks to respond, though most questions are answered much sooner.

What to include in your email

To help us resolve your issue quickly, please include:

  • The email address associated with your ShieldVolt account
  • A short description of what happened, and what you expected instead
  • Screenshots, if the issue is visual
  • Your device model and iOS version (Settings → General → About)

Frequently asked questions

I purchased a Claim Weapon Report but can't access it. What do I do?

Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the purchase. If the report still doesn't appear, email us your Apple ID purchase receipt and the claim it was linked to, and we'll investigate.

My claim report was archived but I'm still working on my claim. Can I get it back?

Yes. Archived reports can be reactivated at any time from within the app for a reduced reactivation price. Your previous calculations and scripts remain visible either way.

How do I cancel my Peace of Mind subscription?

Subscriptions are managed entirely through Apple, not within ShieldVolt. Go to Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone, select ShieldVolt, and cancel from there.

I disconnected my Tesla account. Will this affect my claims?

No. Disconnecting Tesla account access only affects the Renewal Timing Alert feature. Your claims, calculations, and reports are stored separately and are not affected.

Can I request a refund?

All purchases are billed through Apple. Refund requests are handled by Apple, not ShieldVolt directly. You can request one at reportaproblem.apple.com.
