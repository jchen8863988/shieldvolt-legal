1. Overview

ShieldVolt ("ShieldVolt," "we," "us," or "our") is an independently operated mobile application that helps Tesla vehicle owners understand insurance quotes, calculate fair claim values, and navigate the insurance claims process. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and the choices you have.

ShieldVolt is operated by an individual developer based in the United States. By using the app, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide directly

  • Account information: email address, and any name you choose to provide.
  • Vehicle and claim details: your Tesla model, VIN (if provided), state of residence, insurer name, claim numbers, incident dates, and damage descriptions you enter.
  • Accident evidence: photos, videos, dashcam clips, written notes, and other party's contact/insurance information that you choose to upload when documenting an incident.
  • Support communications: anything you send us when you contact shieldvolt@familymedvault.com for help.

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • Usage data: which features you open and how the app is used, to help us fix bugs and improve reliability.
  • Device identifiers: a randomly generated identifier tied to your account, used to keep your data separated from other users' data.
  • Purchase history: records of in-app purchases and subscription status, provided to us by Apple (see Section 5).

2.3 Information from Tesla (if you connect your account)

If you choose to connect your Tesla account, we receive limited vehicle data through Tesla's official Fleet API, including your vehicle's Safety Score and basic vehicle identifiers, used solely to power the Renewal Timing Alert feature. See Section 4 for details.

3. How We Share Information With Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with service providers that help us operate the app, as described below.

RecipientWhat is sharedPurpose
OpenAI Claim details, damage descriptions, vehicle model, state, and policy information you enter into the Quote Decode, Claim Calculator, Adjuster Script, and related AI features To generate quote explanations, claim estimates, and negotiation scripts. OpenAI processes this data to return a response and does not use it to train their models under our API terms.

We do not use advertising networks or sell data to data brokers. ShieldVolt does not display third-party ads and does not share your information for advertising purposes.

4. Tesla Vehicle Data

ShieldVolt's Renewal Timing Alert feature is optional. If you choose to connect your Tesla account, you will be redirected to Tesla's own authorization page, where you grant ShieldVolt limited, revocable access to specific vehicle data (such as your Safety Score) through Tesla's official Fleet API. We do not receive your Tesla account password. You can disconnect this access at any time from within the app or directly through your Tesla account settings.

5. Purchases and Subscriptions

All payments in ShieldVolt are processed by Apple through the App Store's in-app purchase system. We do not collect or store your payment card details — Apple handles all billing. We receive only the transaction confirmation needed to unlock the feature you purchased (such as a Claim Weapon Report or a Peace of Mind subscription).

6. Data Retention

We retain your account and claim data for as long as your account is active. Claim Weapon Reports follow the lifecycle described in the app: an active report is automatically archived after the claim is marked resolved, or after 365 days, whichever comes first. Archived data remains viewable in your account history unless you request deletion.

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us (Section 12). We will delete your data within a reasonable time, except where we are required to retain certain records (for example, transaction records) to comply with applicable law.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent for Tesla account connection at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email shieldvolt@familymedvault.com. We will respond within a reasonable time and in any case no later than the timeframe required by applicable law.

8. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards, including encrypted data transmission and access-controlled database storage, to protect your information. No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

ShieldVolt is not directed to children under the age of 16 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

10. International Users

ShieldVolt is operated from the United States. If you use the app from outside the United States, your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in your country.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you within the app or by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of ShieldVolt after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
